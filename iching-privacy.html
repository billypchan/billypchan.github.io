Privacy Policy — 周易占卜 (I Ching)

Last updated: 26 April 2026 · Effective for the Android app under package com.billypchan.iching and the iOS counterpart.

This page describes what data the 周易占卜 (I Ching) mobile app collects, how it is used, and what choices you have. The app is published by Bill Chan as an individual developer.

1. What we collect

On your device only (never sent to us):

  • Divination history — the timestamp, hexagram, and (if you grant the permission) approximate location of each divination you cast. Stored in the app's local database. You can delete history at any time from inside the app, and it is removed when you uninstall.
  • Settings — your text-to-speech language preference and similar choices. Stored locally.

Sent to third parties:

  • Advertising identifier (Android Advertising ID / iOS IDFA) and standard ad-request metadata are collected by Google AdMob to serve banner ads inside the app. Google's handling of this data is governed by the Google Privacy Policy. You can reset or limit your advertising ID from your device settings.

What we do not collect: name, email, phone number, contacts, photos, microphone audio, calendar, files, account login, payment details. The app has no sign-up flow.

2. Why we use the location permission

The app requests the optional ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION / ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION permission so it can record where you cast a divination, alongside the time. This is part of traditional divination practice and is stored only in your local divination history. The location is never transmitted off your device, never used for advertising, and never shared with any third party. Denying the permission has no effect on the rest of the app.

3. Ads

The free version of the app shows banner ads served by Google AdMob. Ads are not personalised based on data we hold (we don't hold any), but Google may personalise them based on data Google itself has collected. For users in the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom, and other jurisdictions where consent is required, the AdMob SDK presents a Google-provided consent form on first launch.

4. Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 (or the equivalent local minimum age). We do not knowingly collect data from children. The Play Store target-audience setting is 13+.

5. Data retention

All data collected on your device stays on your device for as long as the app is installed. Uninstalling the app deletes all local data. Data sent to Google AdMob is retained according to Google's policies.

6. Your choices

  • Deny or revoke the location permission from system settings.
  • Reset your advertising identifier from system settings.
  • Delete divination history from inside the app.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data.

7. Changes to this policy

If we make a material change we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after a change constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

Questions, concerns, or data requests: billypchan@gmail.com

隱私權政策 — 周易占卜

更新日期：2026 年 4 月 26 日 · 適用於 Android 套件 com.billypchan.iching 與相對應之 iOS 版本。

本頁面說明周易占卜 App 收集哪些資料、如何使用，以及您可以做的選擇。本 App 由 Bill Chan 以個人開發者身份發行。

一、我們收集的資料

僅儲存於您的裝置（絕不上傳）：

  • 占卜紀錄：每次起卦的時間、卦象，以及（在您授權的情況下）所在位置。儲存於 App 內之本機資料庫，您可隨時於 App 內刪除，移除 App 即一併清除。
  • 設定：語音語言等使用者偏好，僅存於本機。

傳送至第三方：

  • 廣告識別碼（Android Advertising ID / iOS IDFA）以及標準廣告請求資料，由 Google AdMob 收集以提供 App 內橫幅廣告。Google 對此資料之處理依 Google 隱私權政策。您可在裝置設定中重設或限制廣告識別碼。

我們不收集：姓名、電郵、電話、通訊錄、相片、麥克風音訊、行事曆、檔案、登入資訊、付款資料。App 不需註冊。

二、為何需要位置權限

App 請求選用之 ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION / ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION 權限，以記錄起卦當下所在位置，與時間一同保存於本機之占卜歷史。此為傳統占卜記錄習慣，位置資料絕不離開您的裝置，亦不用於廣告或任何第三方分享。拒絕此權限不影響其他功能。

三、廣告

免費版本以 Google AdMob 提供橫幅廣告。本 App 本身不持有用以個人化廣告的資料，但 Google 可能依其自有資料進行個人化。歐洲經濟區、英國等需取得同意之地區，AdMob SDK 將於首次啟動時呈現 Google 提供之同意視窗。

四、兒童

本 App 不以 13 歲（或當地法定最低年齡）以下兒童為對象，亦不刻意收集兒童資料。Play 商店之目標年齡層設為 13 歲以上。

五、資料保留

所有本機資料於 App 安裝期間皆保留於您的裝置，移除 App 即一併刪除。送至 Google AdMob 之資料依 Google 政策保留。

六、您的選擇

  • 於系統設定拒絕或撤回位置權限。
  • 於系統設定重設廣告識別碼。
  • 於 App 內刪除占卜歷史。
  • 移除 App 以清除所有本機資料。

七、政策變更

若有重大變更，本頁面頂端「更新日期」將同步更新。變更後繼續使用 App 即視為接受新版政策。
